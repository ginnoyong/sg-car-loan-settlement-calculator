How SG early settlement works
Cash purchase = car price + finance rebate (rebate was never given since no loan taken)
Gross interest return = remaining interest via Rule of 78 tail weights
Early settlement fee on interest refund = gross interest return × ES fee % on interest refund (bank keeps this)
Net interest return = gross interest return × (1 − ES fee % on interest refund)
Other early settlement penalty = penalty % × remaining principal (or original loan amount)
Other penalty fees = other early settlement penalty % × remaining principal (or original loan amount)
Settlement payoff = remaining principal + ES fee on interest refund + other penalty fees + finance rebate clawback
Total car cost (loan) = down payment + loan principal + cumulative interest paid + ES fee on interest refund + other penalty fees + finance rebate clawback
Saves vs full term = total car cost at full term − total car cost if settle this month
