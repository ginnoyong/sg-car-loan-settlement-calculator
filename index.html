How SG early settlement works
Cash purchase = car price + finance rebate (rebate was never given since no loan taken)
Step 1 — Gross interest rebate = remaining interest via Rule of 78 tail weights
Step 2 — Admin clawback = gross interest rebate × admin clawback %  (bank keeps this)
Step 3 — Net interest rebate = gross interest rebate × (1 − admin clawback %)
Step 4 — Early settlement fee = fee % × remaining principal (or original loan amount)
Step 5 — Settlement payoff = remaining principal + admin clawback amount + early settlement fee + finance rebate clawback
Total car cost (loan) = down payment + loan principal + cumulative interest paid + admin clawback + ES fee + finance rebate clawback
